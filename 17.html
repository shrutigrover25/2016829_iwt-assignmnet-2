AMAN GUPTA

Aman Gupta Biography: Aman Gupta is the Co-founder and CMO of boAt. He is among the seven Sharks of Shark Tank India, the Indian adaptation of the American business reality show Shark Tank.

Aman Gupta attended Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram and is an alumnus of Delhi University. He later cleared CA entrance and attended the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. To realise his dream of becoming an entrepreneur, Aman Gupta pursued an MBA in Finance and Strategy from the Indian School of Business. He did his second MBA in general management and marketing from Kellogg Graduate School of Management, USA.
